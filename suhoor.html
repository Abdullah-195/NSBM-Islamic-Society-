Suhoor Meal Distribution

Alhamdulillah, we are organizing Suhoor meal distribution during Ramadan specifically for students staying at hostels and boarding houses near the university.

Program Details

To ensure no student misses their Suhoor, the NSBM Islamic Society is facilitating meal deliveries for those residing away from home.

  • Timing: Delivery will be coordinated between 3:00 AM - 4:00 AM.
  • Coverage: Selected hostels and boarding houses around Pitipana / Homagama.
  • Meals: Nutritious locally prepared Suhoor meals.
  • Cost: Subsidized or fully covered (please see form details).

Want to Contribute?

If you would like to sponsor a Suhoor meal for a fellow student, please contact our community outreach team.
